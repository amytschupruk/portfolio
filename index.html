About me

who i am

Hi! My name is Amy Tschupruk and I’m a Toronto-based web developer who loves learning new skills and making beautiful, functional sites. I really like the process of collaborating and bouncing off ideas as a team to produce results that everyone involved can be proud of. My visual arts background has given me an eye for recreating designs and thinking creatively, while my prior career as an insurance adjuster (cause arting ain’t gonna pay the bills) gave me experience working under pressure with strict deadlines. When I’m not coding, you can find me drawing, enjoying live music (the louder the better), and unapologetically taking instagram photos of my food. I completed the HackerYou web development Bootcamp in June 2017, and can’t wait to dive into the field expand my coding skills!
